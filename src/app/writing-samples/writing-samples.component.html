Writing Samples

Below you fill find a few examples of my projects that I created for the Hospitality Catering and Event Management class that was in charge of planning the Hospitality Gala 2020. Through these projects I have learned so much about communication and presentation. The writing samples include my group and my color scheme inspiration document that ended up being chosen as the Gala 2020 color scheme, a centerpiece assignment that required us to find inspiration for our arrangements and quotes from different vendors, and lastly our MoodBoard that we created to present to the leadership team in hopes that it will be incorporated into the Gala for 2020.
